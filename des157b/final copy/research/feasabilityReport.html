Coding Components & Feasability Report

Features + Technology

  • Sign up / login to your account - Google Account sign in option, or Back4App storage for usernames and passwords
  • View different photo categories and click on photos to read their captions - Masonry Library for photo layouts & Animate On Scroll Library for viewing individual photos
  • Upload your own photo with an optional caption - Back4App
  • Delete your photo + caption - Back4App
  • Change your password - Back4App, not for Google Sign Ups
  • Responsive design - media queries

Assessment

  • I have used Google authentication before to have users log into their accounts, so I feel confident to do it again, but I will need to refresh on the small details. I have yet to use Back4App for manual sign up/login authentication but I believe I'll be able to learn it.
  • I have not used Masonry Library before, but I have used Grid to display text and images, so I'm hoping it's somewhat similar. If not, the documentation will be enough to learn.
  • I have used the Animate On Scroll library and just need to make sure it will work with other libraries.
  • I have previously developed mobile and desktop designs and will start off with mobile first.
