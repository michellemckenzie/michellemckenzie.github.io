Game of Pig

Instructions:

There are two players. A player will be randomly selected to begin the game. Players will take turns rolling two "die".

If one dice lands on a 1, the current user's turn ends, and the other player gets to play. If a player rolls two 1s, that's called snake eyes, and the current player's score goes to 0, and the other player rolls.

The first player to reach the goal points, or more, is the winner!
