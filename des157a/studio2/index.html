memories

My boyfriend and I were riding our electric scooters around Davis and I really wanted to go to the sunflower field. The sun was setting and our batteries were getting low, so it was a race against time, and how fast we could kick off our scooters to get this picture.

This was the first time I went kayaking. Carrying the kayak from the car to the lake was exhausting, but worth it. I would only do it during the summer though because water gets in everywhere.

My friends had shown up to my house to surprise me with presents and a cake for my 17th birthday. It meant a lot to me since my birthday is on Christmas and I never get to see friends that day.

Koda is a sweet Australian Shepherd if he knows you have food. Otherwise he’s a rambunctious dog with an infinite amount of energy. You can see my shadow holding a treat up to keep him still long enough to capture his cuteness.

Snapped a photo of my friend Alicia at Lake Tahoe. She woke up early that day to go parasailing with her family. After a cool swim in the crystal water, she’s finally relaxing in the warm sun.

A group of people are lined up on a field. They’re encouraging the other people on the bleachers to join them in a game of soccer.

As a kid, I had a blue Volkswagen Beetle, toy car. It was always my choice for playtime with my younger brother. Now I have a turbo charged Beetle.

I went on a hike at a place called Cronan Ranch and came across this old looking building. But here’s a surprise, it was built in 2003 for a movie - Love Comes Softly.
